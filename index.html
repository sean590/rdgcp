Track Record

Companies we've built

Our leadership team has founded, built, and scaled technology and telecommunications companies from inception through successful exits.

Wurl

Created a leading platform for streaming television distribution, connecting content providers with connected TV platforms and powering ad-supported streaming at scale.

Progress Telecom

Built a fiber-optic telecommunications network providing enterprise connectivity and infrastructure services across the southeastern United States.

TowerCloud

Developed fiber-based backhaul infrastructure connecting cell towers to carrier networks, enabling the expansion of mobile broadband capacity nationwide.

@Home Network

Pioneered high-speed internet delivery over cable infrastructure, becoming one of the first broadband internet service providers in the United States.
